‚è±Ô∏è 20-45 minutes üìä Win-win deals create referrals

Negotiation isn't a battle‚Äîit's a collaborative process to find a deal that works for everyone. Use the L.A.R.C. method for objections: Listen, Acknowledge, Respond, Close.

StepAction
L ‚Äî ListenLet them finish completely. Don't interrupt or get defensive.
A ‚Äî Acknowledge"I completely understand how you feel..." Validates their concern.
R ‚Äî RespondAnswer with value, not just concessions. Why is this fair?
C ‚Äî Close"Does that address your concern? Are we ready to move forward?"

[Respond]

üí° Silence is Golden

‚ö†Ô∏è Don't Give Without Getting
If you make a concession, get a commitment: "If I can get you X, are you ready to move forward today?"

Objections are not rejections‚Äîthey're requests for more information. Use the L.A.R.C. method to handle any objection professionally.

The L.A.R.C. Method

StepWhat To DoExample
L ‚Äî ListenLet them finish. Don't interrupt.Nod, maintain eye contact, take notes.
A ‚Äî AcknowledgeValidate their concern."I completely understand how you feel..."
R ‚Äî RespondAddress with value or solution.Provide information, build value, offer alternatives.
C ‚Äî CloseAsk for next step."Does that address your concern? Ready to move forward?"

Common Objections & Responses

[Acknowledge]

[Respond]

[Close]

[Close]
